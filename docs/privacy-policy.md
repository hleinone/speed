# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Speed is a simple app for displaying the current speed of the device using the device GPS sensor.

## Data We Use

Speed uses location data from your device to calculate and display your current speed.

The app may request access to precise location because accurate speed measurement requires precise GPS data.

Speed also stores your selected speed unit, such as kilometers per hour or miles per hour, on your device.

## How Data Is Processed

Location data is processed on your device.

Speed does not transmit your location data to any server.

Speed does not create user accounts.

Speed does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Speed does not sell or share personal data.

## Data Retention

Speed does not store location history.

Your selected speed unit is stored locally on your device. You can remove this local preference by uninstalling the app or clearing the app data.

## Third Parties

Speed does not send your personal data to third parties.

## Children

Speed is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be published on this page.

## Contact
